ABOUT US

"Our mission is to share the message of Christ’s love by serving those in need, nurturing faith, and fostering a global community rooted in compassion, grace, and service."

Overview

Found Souls for Christ is a faith-driven charity dedicated to spreading the love of Jesus through compassionate outreach and humanitarian aid. Our mission is to serve those in need, nurture faith, and build a global community rooted in compassion, grace, and service. Through various programs, we provide both material support and spiritual guidance to uplift and empower individuals and communities. Founded on the belief that every soul matters, we strive to be the hands and feet of Christ, bringing hope and love to those we serve.

Our Mission

Our mission at Found Souls for Christ is to embody the love of Jesus by offering compassionate outreach and support to those in need. We aim to nurture faith, promote spiritual growth, and create a community rooted in grace and service. Through our actions, we strive to bring hope, healing, and the message of Christ to every soul we encounter.

Our Story

Found Souls For Christ is a voluntary organisation founded by Mr and Mrs Krampah for the purpose of people's physical and spiritual needs. This organisation provides the needs of the needy and provides humanitarian support to the needy. We provide school fees to the less privileged, visit hospitals, give support to children and nursing mothers, support and encourage drug addicts. For the spiritual aspect, we propagate the gospel, healing and deliverance.
